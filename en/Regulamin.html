​Brokatowe Domki Rules and Regulations

​ ​Making a reservation and commencing a stay at Brokatowe Domki signifies that all guests have read and accepted these rules and regulations. The rules apply to all guests staying on the premises.

1

​The overnight stay period lasts from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM (check-in) and ends at 10:00 AM (check-out).

2

​Quiet hours are in effect from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM.

3

​​Visitors are allowed to stay on the premises only until 10:00 PM.

4

​Parking – Each cottage is provided with one parking space in the unguarded parking lot on the premises.

5

​Any comments regarding damages, destruction, or missing items should be reported immediately to the owner.

6

​Failure to report within 1 hour of key collection means that guests have no objections regarding the condition of the cottage and the quantity and quality of the equipment.

7​

​Guests bear full financial responsibility for damages caused by their fault to the cottage or its equipment.

8

​The owner of the property is not responsible for guest injuries, damages, destruction, or theft of guests' movable property.

9

​Children and youth under 18 years of age are allowed on the premises only under adult supervision.

10

​Smoking of cigarettes and other tobacco products is prohibited in the cottages.

11

​The organization of parties and loud gatherings that disturb the stay of other guests is prohibited.

12

​The use of electrical or gas appliances that are not part of the cottage's equipment is prohibited.

13

​It is prohibited to bring explosives, flammable, or dangerous materials onto the property.

14

​In the event of a gross violation of the regulations, the Owner reserves the right to immediately terminate the stay without any refund.

15

​The stay of pets is allowed only after prior agreement with the owner.

16

​The guest is required to notify about bringing a pet at the time of booking

17

​The pet owner is responsible for any damages as well as for maintaining cleanliness after their pet.

18

​Pets must be kept on a leash and are not allowed on beds or furniture intended for guests.

19

​The fee for a pet is 30 PLN per day.

20

​The cottage is intended for the specific number of people indicated in the reservation.

21

​Waste segregation and maintaining cleanliness inside the cabins and throughout the property are mandatory.

22

​Upon check-out, Guests are required to leave the cabin in a condition close to that which they found it, including, but not limited to:​
- The bin must be emptied,
- All dishes must be washed,
- The refrigerator must be empty,
- The interior must be generally tidy.
